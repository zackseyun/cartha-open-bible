Aᵇᵒᵈᵉᶠᵍ ²⁶ c corrupt as context shows. As the
sun looks down on mire and is not defiled but rather dries it up
and banishes its evil odour, so the pure mind though beset with the
defilements of earth is not itself defiled but rather buildeth them up!
Here we require a word signifying the cleansing action of the pure
mind on the pollutions of the world. Hence יבנה (= οἰκοδομεῖ) may be
corrupt for יזכה = καθαρίζει.
