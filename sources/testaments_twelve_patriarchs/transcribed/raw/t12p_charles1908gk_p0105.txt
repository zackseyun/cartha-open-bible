either a bad rendering or a corruption of παραιτούμενος. See parallel
text in Test. Dan. vi. 2. Finally A = φύλαξ (or προιστάμενος?) τοῦ
γένους and S¹ σώζων τ. γ., which are nearer to d than to β-d. In
fact d, A imply the same original; for պահապան (= φύλαξ) is corrupt
for պաշտպան = ὑπερασπιστής. On the other hand, A supports h, β-d
in the parallel passage in Test. Dan. vi. 2. After γένος adg add τοῦ.
